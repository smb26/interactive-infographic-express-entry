Express Entry

Express Entry System is known as the most promising and applicable for International Student who wants to become a Permanent Resident of Canada (and earn Canada Citizenship later on).

Express Entry is a points-based system used to manage and sorting applications for permanent residence (PR) from several immigration programs.

Comprehensive Ranking system

All candidates from all immigration programs under Express Entry stream will be put in to a pool and will be sorted base on their total point in Comprehensive Ranking System (CRS point).

There are a lot of factors that affect on your CRS point such as age, education, skill, work experience, language ability, etc. Each factor has different amount of points and the number of point you can get from a factor depends on your "level". I.e: You get 150 points if you have a PhD degree, but you only get 120 points if you only have Bachelor degree.

The factor that has the largest CRS points (600 out of total 1,200) is being nominated by a Province or Territory via Province Nominate Program (PNP).

Invitation to Apply

Almost twice a month, the government selects the highest-ranking candidates from the pool and invite them to apply for permanent resident status by sending them the Invitation to Apply (ITA) letter.
